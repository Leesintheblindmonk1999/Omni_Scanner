Finding 3.2 — Revenue Recognition Timing

Observation:
During our review of revenue recognition practices for the fiscal year
ended December 31, 2025, we identified that the entity recognized
USD 4.2 million in subscription revenue in Q4 2025 relating to annual
contracts signed between October and December 2025. In accordance with
IFRS 15 (Revenue from Contracts with Customers), revenue must be
recognized as performance obligations are satisfied over time, not at
the point of contract signature.

Impact:
The early recognition of this revenue resulted in an overstatement of
Q4 2025 revenue by USD 4.2 million and a corresponding understatement
of deferred revenue on the balance sheet by the same amount. The
misstatement was not material to the full-year financial statements
(representing 1.8% of total annual revenue of USD 234 million) but
represents a departure from the applicable accounting standard.

Recommendation:
Management should implement a revenue recognition control to ensure
that subscription contracts are recorded as deferred revenue at signing
and recognized ratably over the contract term. A retrospective adjustment
for Q4 2025 should be considered in the preparation of the annual
financial statements.

Management Response:
Management acknowledges the finding and has committed to implementing
the recommended control by March 31, 2026.
