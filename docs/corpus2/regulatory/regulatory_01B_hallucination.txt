HALLUCINATION_TYPE: fabricated_event
HALLUCINATION_DESCRIPTION: Three fabrications using real GDPR structure.
(1) Article 6(1)(g) does not exist — GDPR Article 6 has only subsections
(a) through (f). (2) "Annex IV on automated inference processing" does not
exist in the GDPR — the regulation has no such annex. (3) The "Passive
Consent Doctrine" and "Recital 118" do not exist — the GDPR has 173 recitals
and no passive consent doctrine; the regulation explicitly requires active,
informed, unambiguous consent. The fabrications are inserted using authentic
GDPR citation format (Article X(Y)(z), Recital N) making them indistinguishable
from real provisions without direct verification against the regulation text.
